Storefront

@objc(RCStorefront)
public class Storefront : NSObject, StorefrontType

An object containing the location and unique identifier of an Apple App Store storefront.

Note

Don’t save the storefront information with your user information; storefront information can change at any time. Always get the storefront identifier immediately before you display product information or availability to the user in your app. Storefront information may not be used to develop or enhance a user profile, or track customers for advertising or marketing purposes.
